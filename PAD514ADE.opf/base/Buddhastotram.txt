buddhastotramom namo buddhāyanamo'stu buddhāya viśuddhabodhayeviśuddhadharmapratibhāsabuddhaye
|saddharmapuṇyopagatānubuddhayebhavāgraśūnyāya viśuddhabuddhaye

aho aho buddhamanantatejasaṃaho aho sāgaramerugulmam |aho aho buddhamanantagocaramaudumbaraṃ
puṣpamivātidurlabham

aho mahākāruṇikastathāgataḥaho aho śākyakulasya ketuḥ |aho praṇamyaḥ sa narendrasūryoyenedṛśaṃ
bhāṣitasūtramuttamam

anugrahārthaṃ sa hi sarvasattvānsaddharmacaryāmupadiśya nūnam |śānteśvaraḥ śākyamunistathāgataḥsattvottamaḥ
śāntapuraṃ praviṣṭaḥ

gambhīraśāntaṃ virajāsamādhi-padaṃ praviṣṭe jinabuddhagocare |śūnyāśca kāyāstvatha śrāvakāṇāṃśūnyā
vihārā dvipadottamānām

te sarvadharmā prakṛtiśca śūnyāsattvā hi śūnyā nahi jātu vidyate

nityaṃ ca nityaṃ ca jinaṃ smarāminityaṃ ca śocāmi jinasya darśanam |nityaṃ ca nityaṃ praṇidhiṃ
karomisaṃbuddhapūjyasya ca darśanārtham

sthāpyeha nityaṃ dharaṇīṣu jānuśokābhitapto jinadarśanāya |odantakāruṇyavināyakastva-matīva
tṛṣṇā sugatasya darśane

satāṃ varo yadvijahāra nityaṃdadāhi me darśanatoyaśītalam |sattvāḥ satṛṣṇāstava rūpadarśaneprahlādayainān
karuṇājalena

kāruṇyabhāvaṃ kuru nātha nāyakadadāhi me darśanaṃ saumyarūpam |tvaṃ pāhi trātarjagadekadeśitāśūnyāśca
kāyāstvatha śrāvakāṇām

ākāśatulyā gaganasvabhāvāmāyāmarīcyodakacandrakāyāḥ |sarve ca sattvāḥ sukhitasvabhāvābhavantu
bho nāyaka tvatprasādāt

bodhisattvasamuccayānāma kuladevatāviracitaṃbuddhastotraṃ samāptam ||
